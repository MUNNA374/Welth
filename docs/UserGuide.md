# Welth User Manual

Welcome to Welth! Here is how to navigate and manage your personal finance dashboard.

## Onboarding
1. Go to `http://localhost:3000/register` to register an account.
2. Log in using your email and password.

## Dashboard Overview
The main screen lists:
- **Net Worth & Cash Flow**: Beautiful visual charts displaying monthly progress.
- **Budget Alerter**: Progress indicators outlining when you are approaching limits.
- **XGBoost Spending Forecasts**: Smart forecasting values telling you tomorrow's estimated outflows.

## Receipt OCR Uploads
Go to the **Transactions** page, click **Scan Receipt**, and upload an image. The AI parses store name, categories, and totals automatically.
