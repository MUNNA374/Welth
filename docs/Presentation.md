# Welth Project Presentation

## Slide 1: Introduction
- **Title**: Welth personal finance platform
- **Subtitle**: AI-powered conversational budgeting and ML forecasting
- **Presenter**: Developer Team

## Slide 2: The Problem
- Modern users lack consolidated views of checking accounts, credit balances, and stock portfolio performance.
- Traditional budgeting is rigid and requires tedious manual entry.

## Slide 3: The Solution — Welth
- Consolidation of assets, loans, insurance, and subscriptions.
- Gemini AI OCR receipt scanner and auto-categorization.
- XGBoost forecasting of spending patterns.

## Slide 4: Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: Next.js 15, React 19, TypeScript, Recharts, Framer Motion
- **Backend**: FastAPI, Celery, Redis, Pydantic, Prisma ORM
- **Database**: PostgreSQL
- **AI/ML**: Google Gemini AI, XGBoost, Scikit-learn
